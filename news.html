ICTAM Women-led ICT Firm of the Year (2025) Nomination

By Admin • Oct 27, 2025 • 2 min read

We were proud to announce that our company had been nominated for the “Women-led ICT Firm of the Year (2025)” award by the ICT Association of Malawi (ICTAM). This recognition highlighted our continued dedication to innovation, excellence, and leadership in the ICT sector.

The nomination celebrated not only our team’s hard work and creativity, but also the vision and resilience of women who led the digital transformation agenda in Malawi and beyond. Through our projects, partnerships, and community-driven initiatives, we remained committed to leveraging technology to create sustainable impact—empowering businesses, improving digital literacy, and driving inclusive growth.

This acknowledgment from ICTAM reaffirmed our belief that when women are given equal opportunities to lead and innovate, entire communities thrive. We took pride in being part of a growing movement of women entrepreneurs who were shaping the future of Malawi’s ICT landscape—bridging digital gaps, creating opportunities, and inspiring the next generation of tech leaders.

We extended our heartfelt appreciation to ICTAM for the recognition, to our clients and partners for their continued trust, and to our dedicated team whose commitment and passion made everything possible. As we looked ahead, the nomination inspired us to continue pushing boundaries, embracing creativity, and delivering digital solutions that made a real difference.

Together, we rose. Together, we innovated. Together, we transformed Malawi’s digital future.

Nomination for the Outstanding Woman in Technology Award

By Admin • 2024 • 3 min read

We were delighted to share that our Managing Director, Phyllis Manguluti, had been nominated for the Outstanding Woman in Technology Award (2024) at the SheMillionaire Africa Women’s Wealth Builders Awards.

This nomination recognized her remarkable leadership in transforming technology services and her unwavering commitment to advancing women’s participation in STEM fields. Through her vision and dedication, Phyllis continued to inspire innovation within our organization and beyond—proving that empowered women truly empower others.

Her recognition was not only a personal achievement but also a reflection of the collective progress of women breaking barriers in Malawi’s ICT sector. Her advocacy for inclusivity, mentorship, and digital empowerment has helped create more opportunities for young women aspiring to thrive in technology and entrepreneurship.

We were incredibly proud of this recognition and grateful to SheMillionaire Africa for celebrating women who are driving meaningful change across the continent.

Congratulations once again to our Managing Director, Phyllis Manguluti—an inspiring leader and true trailblazer in technology.

Finalist and Winner of the PAWES Women in Innovation, Engineering, Science & Technology Award

By Admin • Dec 22, 2024 • 1 min read

We were proud to share that our Managing Director, Phyllis Manguluti, was recognised not just as a finalist but as the winner of the Women in Innovation, Engineering, Science & Technology Award at the Pan African Women Empowerment Summit (PAWES) 2024.

This accolade celebrated her outstanding contributions towards inclusive innovation and digital empowerment — through local technology solutions that met real business challenges across the region. Her win underscored a commitment to creating systems that empower women and transform businesses, particularly in contexts where digital access and gender equity are key.

Being honoured at PAWES was a significant milestone — a powerful recognition of Phyllis’s leadership, vision and impact in the fields of STEM and innovation in Malawi and beyond. We are deeply honoured by this achievement and remain dedicated to advancing innovation that is inclusive, high-impact and locally rooted.

Congratulations to our Managing Director, Phyllis Manguluti, for her well-deserved win and for continuing to lead the way in technology, innovation and empowerment.

MCCCI Business Voice Magazine Feature

By Admin • Dec 22, 2024 • 1 min read

We were proud to share that our Managing Director, Phyllis Manguluti, was featured on the cover of MCCCI Business Voice Magazine (2024).

This feature recognized her exceptional leadership in spearheading digital transformation and setting new standards in client engagement through innovative technology solutions across Malawi’s public and private sectors. Phyllis’s work has not only advanced the adoption of modern ICT practices but also inspired organizations to rethink how technology can drive efficiency, inclusivity, and growth.

Being highlighted by MCCCI Business Voice Magazine was a testament to Phyllis’s vision, her commitment to excellence, and her influence as a thought leader in Malawi’s technology and business landscape.

Congratulations to Phyllis Manguluti for continuing to lead with innovation, impact, and inspiration.
